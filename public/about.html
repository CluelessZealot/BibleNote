About Bible Note

Bible Note is a simple web-based tool designed to help users jot down and organize their personal reflections, notes, and devotionals based on Bible passages.

Whether you're preparing for a study, recording a prayer journal, or saving your favorite verses, Bible Note provides an easy and intuitive way to capture your thoughts.

Features

  • Note-taking by Book, Chapter, and Verse
  • Devotional journaling
  • Simple and clean interface
  • Easy text upload support (coming soon)
